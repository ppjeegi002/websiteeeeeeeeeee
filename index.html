I would describe myself as a hardworking, self-taught developer who has competent proficiency in Java and Python. I have experience in both app and web development. Leadership is something that I feel comes naturally to me - I am the first-year representative for both Enactus DCU and the Indian Society. I love teaching others how to program and have always believed in the upliftment of others with me so I spend my free time teaching kids at CoderDojo how to program. I am extremely passionate about helping others get into computer science because of the misconception that some may have about what it actually is. In the future, I would love to be known as a tech leader who helps others realize their true potential.
